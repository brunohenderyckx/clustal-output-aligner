Step 1 – Design input sequences for Clustal Omega tool

We retrieved amino acid sequences from the NCBI – protein database (https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/protein).



Press “search” to list La protein in the organism Homo sapiens. This will reveal a list of potential matches.



Press “FASTA” to directly link to the amino acid sequence in fasta format compatible with Clustal Omega. All sequences are obtained in fasta format which is a text-based format where the first line contains a “>” followed by a unique identifier and the second line contains the protein, RNA or DNA sequence.
